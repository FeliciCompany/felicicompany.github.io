Our founders

Margaret Johnson (Founder)

Margaret is co-founder and Musical Director of Felici. She trained at the Royal College of Music and has worked with operatic societies, music groups, choirs and soloists, as conductor, musical director and accompanist. She especially enjoys working with the voice, being equally at home in opera, operetta, art song and musical theatre. A retired primary headteacher and lay minister, Margaret teaches singing and works as vocal coach and repetiteur. With her co-founder, Zoe Jasko, she has been involved in organising and devising a wide range of Felici events since the group's inception - from classical concerts and tea afternoons to themed evenings such as Fairies in the Garden.

Zoe Jasko (Founder)

Zoe is an experienced soprano, she has worked professionally as a soprano soloist and has played roles in many amateur theatrical shows. Classically trained, Zoe adores the music of Mozart and as a French graduate she is especially happy when singing the sublime songs of Fauré. Zoe has spent many years producing shows and events in the areas of entertainment and education. As a founding director of Felici, she takes great pleasure in working with the talented Felici performers to deliver creative and high-quality musical entertainment, excellently performed and beautifully presented. Having also worked in education and as mother to four school age children, Zoe believes that offering opportunities to young performers is essential and has been thrilled to work in Felici with some highly talented young people at the onset of their careers.

Four excellent musicians, Margaret Johnson (piano), Zoe Jasko (Soprano), Gordon Hodges (Tenor) and Tim Hobman (Baritone) arranged An Evening with Mozart to raise funds for NKF and the impressive sum of Â£640 was raised.
